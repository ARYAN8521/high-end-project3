Key Insights

Analysis of sales data revealed important patterns and opportunities:

  • Total sales amount to 1.57 million, with Order of 22K and a total profit of 175.26K.
  • The highest sales segment is "Consumer" (48.09%), followed by "Corporate" (32.5%) and "Home" (19.35%).
  • Most payments are made through cash on delivery, followed by online payments, while card payments are the least common.
  • The Western region generates the highest sales, with California contributing the most.
  • Sales peak in September, November, and December, while profits are highest in March, October, and December. Monthly sales and profits increased significantly in 2020 compared to 2019
  • Phones have the highest sales among sub-categories, with chairs, binders, and storage following, while accessories have the lowest sales in the top group Office supplies sell the most by category, more than technology and furniture.
  • Standard shipping is the most used ship mode (0.91M sales), with secondary, first class, and same-day shipping used less frequently.
